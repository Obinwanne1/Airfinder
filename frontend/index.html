How Airfinder Earns — Full Transparency

We believe you deserve to know exactly how we make money. No hidden agenda.

Booking Commission
We earn a small commission from airlines and OTA partners when you complete a booking. This is industry-standard and already included in the fare shown.
Fare Markup
On some routes we buy wholesale fares and add a transparent markup. You'll always see this broken down in the True Cost panel before you pay.
Service Fee
A flat service fee covers our technology and support. It's shown clearly in the price breakdown — never hidden in the total.
B2B Licensing
Travel agencies can license the Airfinder platform to power their own search. This keeps Airfinder profitable without charging you more.
